Who We Are

We're serious
about YouTube.

Four years ago, Dragonfruit set out to change the way the world creates and consumes on YouTube. The result has been bigger than we could have imagined.

Unlike other social media agencies, Dragonfruit refuses to celebrate a creator economy that trades viewers' time for rapid-fire dopamine entertainment.

Instead, we lead a global movement to make YouTube a more valuable and educational place — where viral channels don't just build brands, but create genuine value in viewers' lives.

We work with some of the largest creators and brands on the planet. And we're just getting started.

"We've basically stolen Dragonfruit's playbook when it comes to YouTube." — Ali Abdaal, 6.56M subscribers

What We Look For

We hire for
ownership.

We don't hire people who need to be managed. We hire people who take initiative, think clearly, and move fast.

01
You think in outcomes

You don't ask what to do — you figure it out, make a call, and move. You understand tradeoffs and make decisive calls without waiting for perfect conditions.

02
You communicate clearly

Direct, specific, and confident. You can articulate ideas, pitch concepts, and give honest feedback without dancing around the point.

03
You have high standards

You care deeply about the quality of your work. You notice when something is good and when it's not — and you do something about it.

04
You're hungry to grow

You use every project as a chance to get better. You read, you study, you apply — and you bring that energy to everything you work on.

05
You lift others up

You give real feedback, not just reassurance. You make the people around you better and don't hoard knowledge or credit.

06
You're genuinely excited by YouTube

You understand the platform deeply. You follow creators, study what works, and have real opinions about what makes content great.

Hiring Process

Simple. Fast.
Respectful.

We know your time is valuable. Our process is designed to be clear, efficient, and fair — giving you every chance to show what you can actually do.

01
Application

A simple, straightforward application. No unnecessary hoops — just tell us who you are and what you've done.

02
Initial Interview

A focused conversation to understand your background, experience, and how you think.

03
Short Task

A brief task relevant to the role. We want to see how you actually work — not just how you interview.

04
Final Interview

A deeper conversation with the team. A chance for both sides to confirm it's the right fit.

05
Decision

We move fast. You'll hear back quickly with a clear, direct answer — no ghosting, no delays.

⚡

We respect your time. Our process is intentionally lean — most candidates complete it within two weeks. We communicate at every stage and never leave you wondering where things stand.
